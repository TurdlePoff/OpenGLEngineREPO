Implemented:
	- Terrain generated from a heightmap
	- Moving along heightmap

World Navigation Controls:

Object movement:
	WASD - To move the object

Camera movement:
	U - Up   	 |  J - Down	  | Moving on the y-axis
	H - Left 	 |  K - Right	  | Moving on the x-axis
	Y - Forwards | I - Backwards  | Moving on the z-axis
	
	Mouse left click + drag to rotate the camera view
	
	(Note: The axis of the camera and object do not rotate along with the camera.)
